Web Development Practices


  • Understand the big picture

    • You need to have planning and overview of the project to not write unnecessary codes that will keep you off track.
    One should have a good grasp on what will your project be.

  • Write smarter, not more

    • Less means more. A programmer's code should not duplicate or overproduce codes because it can lead to bugs.
    You should incorporate many concepts such as functions, constructors, loops, etc. to make your code neat.

  • Mobile Friendly as a Requirement

    • Many end-users view your application/site at a mobile phone so also keep in mind that it must have the same dimensions
    or functionality as the one that is viewed in your computer.
